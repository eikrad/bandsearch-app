# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple rule: be respectful, constructive, and inclusive.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in communication.
- Assume good intent.
- Give feedback on ideas and code, not people.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination.
- Public or private intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private security/contact channel with the maintainer.
